   Where is Maderperla based?

We are based in Abu Dhabi, in the United Arab Emirates where our CEO also lives. Her vision was to bring a part of Italy to the Eastern world, where she felt a sense of belonging and connection lived hand in hand, and ‘Made in Italy’ could be cherished and appreciated.

In the UAE there is an incredible sense of style, fashion and all which is ‘beautiful’ is truly celebrated. It is of no surprise that we established here our base! 

Our products are all crafted in Italy - precisely from Amalfi Coast & Tuscany, as you can see from the 'Cuoio di Toscana' authenticity certificate placed in your very own Madreperla round box. Madreperla's signature round box is crafted by hand and comes all the way from Milan to your door.

SHIPPING &  RETURNS

     Where do you ship?

We offer worldwide shipping, excluding Cuba, Burma/Myanmar, Iran, North Korea, Sudan, Syria, and Ukraine.

 

     Is shipping included in the price?

Yes, our prices are inclusive of shipping costs as well as import taxes covered by Madreperla. Your only task is to be ready to collect your beautiful delivery.

 

   How long will it take for my parcel to arrive?

Once your sandals are ready for shipping, our partners DHL will be handling your delivery on Express basis which takes

from 2 to 3 working days.

If you are local to the UAE, it can take less time for your shipping to reach you.

In our products gallery, it is mentioned whether the product is available for immediate shipping or if  it is to be crafted,

which means you can place the order and we will have the sandals made for you.

Waiting time can vary from 7 to 10 days.
